* Entropy

Entropy in information theory: everything is great and happy and, surprising things convey more information.

H = -sum(p(x) * log(p(x)))

Where p(x) is the probability of x.

** Cross-entropy

Cross-entropy is a measure of the difference between two probability distributions.

H(p, q) = -sum(p(x) * log(q(x)))

Where p is the true distribution and q is the predicted distribution.

In deep learning, events are happen or do not happen -> p(x) is either 0 or 1.
